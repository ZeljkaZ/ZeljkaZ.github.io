Who are we?

NZ Fitness is a company specialized in fitness coaching of tennis professionals and enthusiasts. Whether you want to improve your speed, agility, endurance or to mitigate the risk of injury, we are here to help.

Here are the services that we offer:

  • Online 1:1 coaching
  • In-person 1:1 coaching
  • Group trainings for juniors
  • Group trainings for adults
  • Tennis fitness camps
