Homemade Hummus by Fit Foodie

  • 250g cooked chickpeas
  • 60ml tahini
  • 30 ml olive oil
  • 60ml freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 medium garlic clove
  • 1-2 tsp salt
  • 1-2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2-3 Tbsp water or aquafaba

Method: Combine tahini and lemon juice in a food processor and mix for 1 minute. Add the olive oil, garlic, cumin and salt and process for 30 seconds to 1 minute. Add chickpeas and mix for 1-2 minutes until well blended. Add some liquid if needed. Serve and enjoy!
