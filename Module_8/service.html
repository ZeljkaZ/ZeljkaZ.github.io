What Do We Offer For You?

At MyTennisFitness, we understand that no two players are the same. Our Online Personal Training service provides you with a fully customized fitness program designed to meet your specific needs, goals, and tennis level. Whether you’re a junior competitor or a recreational player, you’ll benefit from the personalized attention and expert guidance of our trainers, without ever needing to leave your home or gym.

Initial Consultation
A detailed assessesment of your current fitness level, tennis experience, and specific goals.
Tailored Fitness Plan
Personalized program focused on improving your strength, core stability, explosiveness, and injury prevention.
Regular Check-Ins
Stay on track with ongoing feedback and adjustments to your plan as you progress.
24/7 Support
Get answers to your questions and guidance when you need it most, with full access to your trainer.
