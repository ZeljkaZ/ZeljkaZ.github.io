Your Privacy Matters to Us

At MyTennisFitness, we are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data when you visit our website or use our services.


  1. Information We Collect
    • Personal Information We may collect the following personal information when you interact with our website or services:
      • Name
      • Email address
      • Phone number
      • Billing and shipping address
      • Payment details (for purchases)
      • Fitness-related information (for personal training services)
    • Automatically Collected Information When you visit our website, we may collect certain data automatically, including:
      • IP address
      • Browser type and version
      • Time zone settings
      • Operating system
      • Device information
      • Pages visited on our website
  2. How We Use Your Information
    • To Provide Services - We use your personal information to deliver the services you request, such as personal training plans, ready-made programs, and merchandise purchases.
    • To Process Payments - Your payment details are used to process transactions securely.
    • To Communicate With You - We may send you emails about updates, offers, or changes to our services. You can opt-out of these communications at any time.
    • To Improve Our Website - We use automatically collected data to analyze and enhance our website’s performance and functionality.
  3. Data Security
    • We take the security of your data seriously. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or damage. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.
  4. Data Sharing
    • Third-Party Service Providers - We may share your data with third-party service providers for processing payments, sending emails, or improving website functionality. These providers have access only to the information necessary to perform their functions and are required to keep your data confidential.
    • Legal Obligations - We may disclose your personal information if required by law or if we believe such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect our rights, or investigate potential violations of our terms.
  5. Your Data Protection Rights
    • Access: You have the right to request copies of your personal data.
    • Rectification: You have the right to request correction of any inaccurate information.
    • Erasure: You can request that we delete your personal data.
    • Restriction: You can ask us to limit the processing of your data.
    • Objection: You have the right to object to our processing of your data.
    • Data Portability: You can request that we transfer your data to another organisation.
  6. Data Retention
    • We will retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy unless a longer retention period is required by law.
  7. Cookies
    • Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience and analyse website traffic. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.
  8. Third-Party Links
    • Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.
  9. Changes to This Privacy Policy
    • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the updated date will be reflected at the top of the policy.
